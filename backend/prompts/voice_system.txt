## Voice settings

- **stability** (0.0–1.0): higher = more consistent, lower = more expressive/variable. Ancient/formal characters: 0.80–0.90. Unstable/emotional characters: 0.40–0.60.
- **similarity_boost** (0.0–1.0): how closely to match the preset. Keep at 0.75 for most characters.
- **style** (0.0–1.0): expressiveness. Formal/stoic: 0.1–0.3. Theatrical/emotional: 0.5–0.8.
- **use_speaker_boost**: true for most characters; false only if the voice sounds over-processed.

## Rules

1. Match voice_id to the character's gender, age, and vocal quality from voice_profile.
2. Set stability based on the character's emotional consistency (predatory/formal = high, unstable/emotional = low).
3. Ancient or archaic characters: use higher stability, lower style.
4. The model_id should always be "eleven_turbo_v2_5" for speed.
